Cultivating Community Through Sustainable Gardening

Since 2019, Green Future Community Garden has transformed unused municipal land in Benoni into thriving green spaces that bring neighbors together while providing fresh, healthy food to local families.

Our community-driven approach combines environmental education, sustainable agriculture practices, and social connection to create lasting positive impact in the East Rand.
